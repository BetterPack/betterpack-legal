Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the HabitPack application ("Application") collects, uses, and shares personal information of users.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information for your profile, such as your username, display name, and profile picture.
  • Habit and Progress Data: The Application collects information related to your habit tracking, including habit names, goals, progress values, and completion dates.
  • Social Feed Content: If you use the social feed feature, we collect the content you post, including text, images, and videos.
  • Interaction Data: We collect data about your interactions with other users, such as likes and comments.
  • Device Information: We may collect certain information about your device, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How and Where Data is Stored

Your personal information is stored using Firebase services, which run on Google's infrastructure. This includes:

  • Firestore: Your account information, profile data, habit data, social feed content, and interaction data are stored in Firestore, a NoSQL cloud database.
  • Firebase Authentication: Authentication-related information (e.g., email, password hash) is stored by Firebase Authentication.
  • Cloud Storage: Media files (images, videos, profile pictures) are stored in Cloud Storage.

3. Purpose of Data Use

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Application: We use your information to operate the Application and provide you with its features.
  • Personalization: We use your information to personalize your experience.
  • Communication: We may use your email address to send you important notices.
  • Improvement: We use aggregated and anonymized data to analyze Application usage.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Application's features. If you delete your account, we will initiate the process of deleting your personal information.

5. Data Sharing

We share your personal information with third parties only to the extent necessary to operate the Application (e.g., Google/Firebase). We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and request the deletion of your personal information.

7. Delete Account & Delete All Data Flow

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the Application:

  1. Navigate to the Settings section.
  2. Select the Delete Account option.
  3. Confirm your decision to permanently delete your account and data.

Upon confirmation, we will initiate the process of permanently deleting your account and all associated data from our systems. This process is irreversible.

8. Children's Policy

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
