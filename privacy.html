Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the HabitPack application ("Application") collects, uses, and shares personal information of users.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information for your profile, such as your username, display name, and profile picture.
  • Health and Fitness Data: We collect information about your physical activities, including workout logs, exercise duration, specific exercises performed, and habit completion streaks.
  • Social Feed Content (UGC): If you use the social feed feature, we collect the content you voluntarily post, including text, images, and videos. Please note that this content is visible to other users.
  • Interaction Data: We collect data about your interactions with other users, such as likes, comments, and reports.
  • Device Information: We may collect certain information about your device, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for app functionality.

2. How and Where Data is Stored

Your personal information is stored using secure cloud infrastructure services. This includes:

  • Firestore (Google Cloud): Your account information, profile data, habit data, fitness logs, and text-based social content are stored in Firestore.
  • Firebase Authentication: Authentication-related information (e.g., email, password hash) is securely managed by Firebase Authentication.
  • Cloudflare R2: Media files (images, videos, profile pictures) are stored securely using Cloudflare R2 storage services.

3. Purpose of Data Use

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Application: We use your information to operate the Application, track your habits and workouts, and provide social features.
  • Personalization: We use your information to personalize your experience and track your progress.
  • Content Moderation: We review reported User Generated Content to ensure the safety of our community and compliance with our Terms of Service.
  • Communication: We may use your email address to send you important notices regarding your account.
  • Improvement: We use aggregated and anonymized data to analyze Application usage and improve performance.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Application's features. If you delete your account, we will initiate the process of deleting your personal information immediately.

5. Data Sharing

We share your personal information with third-party infrastructure providers only to the extent necessary to operate the Application (specifically Google Firebase and Cloudflare). We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and request the deletion of your personal information at any time.

7. Delete Account & Delete All Data Flow

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the Application:

  1. Navigate to the Settings section.
  2. Select the Delete Account option.
  3. Confirm your decision to permanently delete your account and data.

Request Deletion via Email: If you are unable to access the Application (e.g., you have uninstalled it), you may request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at betteryoubetterword@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request upon verification of ownership.

Upon confirmation, we will permanently delete your account, profile, posts, images, and fitness data from our systems. This process is irreversible.

8. Children's Policy

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: betteryoubetterword@gmail.com
